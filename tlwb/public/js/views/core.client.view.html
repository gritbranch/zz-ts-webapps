The Little Wedding Book

The Little Wedding Book is a one-stop shop wedding website company that provides couples with user-friendly resources and full capabilities at the tip of their finger so that they may prepare for their big day wherever they are, in a way that is streamlined and optimized for a worry-free and stress-free experience during this exciting period of their lives.

Content

In-house content from blogs, inspiration boards, and discussion boards make sure you have all the information you need to know what you want and all the best tips on how to get things done.

Productivity/Organizational Tools

Task manager and budgeting tools make sure you're on top of your tasks and finances. Guest/RSVP tracking and your own wedding website makes sure you have everyone in line with the plans.

Marketplace

Supplier profile matching, communications and booking/reservation systems are in place to make sure you have the best partners fully engaged to make your dream wedding come true.
